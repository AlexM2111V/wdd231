CasaLatina

I chose this name because it blends the warmth of Latin American culture with the comfort of a home away from home. "Casa" means "home" in both Spanish and Portuguese."

Site Purpose

TVivaStay is an all-in-one room rental platform tailored for travelers, students, digital nomads, and professionals looking to rent rooms in the most vibrant cities of South America. The site offers detailed listings of available rooms across major cities like Buenos Aires, Rio de Janeiro, Bogotá, Santiago, Lima, and more.

Scenarios

  • How much does it cost to rent a room in Rio de Janeiro for a week?
  • What’s the weather like in Lima this week? Should I pack for rain?
  • What are the best places to visit during my stay in Montevideo?
